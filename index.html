Crystal Collector

Instructions

At the beginning of the game you are give a random number to try and match your total score too.

Their are four crystals each with a a value between 1 and 12. Click the crystals to discover their value and build your score up to the target number.

But, don't go over or you lose. The fewer crystals collected to reach the target, the higher your score!

The values of each crystal will change at the beggining of the game.
